About Us

NF-Tea is the cutting edge of bubble tea, seamlessly integrating NFT's into your everyday bubble tea experience. We integrate it into as many aspects as we can, such as:

  • Discounts
  • Printed Cups
  • Our own NF-Tea NFTs

Customize your order by having your NFT printed on the top of your lid before your drink is made, and get select Discounts depending on if you own an NFT, and what line your NFT is a part of. By verifying with the blockchain, we guarantee that every order you make is unique to you.
